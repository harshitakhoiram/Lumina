Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lumina?
Lumina is an AI-powered discovery platform designed to help you find your next favorite movie, series, or book. We use intelligent algorithms to sift through thousands of titles to provide a personalized "Recommendation of the Day" just for you.

Whether you're in the mood for a cinematic masterpiece or a gripping novel, Lumina helps you spend less time searching and more time enjoying.
Does Lumina cost anything?
Lumina is currently a free-to-use project developed to showcase the power of AI in content discovery. You can explore movies and books without any subscription fees or hidden costs.
How does the recommendation engine work?
Lumina connects to high-quality external APIs like TMDB for movies and Google Books for literature. Our backend logic analyzes this data against your preferences to suggest content that matches your specific tastes.
Can I save my recommendations?
Yes! By creating a Lumina account, you can save your favorite picks to "My List" and help the AI learn your preferences better over time.
